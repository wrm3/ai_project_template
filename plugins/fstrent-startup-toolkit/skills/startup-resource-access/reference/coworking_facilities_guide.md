# Coworking and Facilities Guide

## Major Coworking Providers

### WeWork
- **Locations**: 700+ globally
- **Costs**: $300-$800/month per person
- **Included**: Desk, wifi, coffee, conference rooms
- **Best For**: Small teams, professional image

### Regus  
- **Locations**: 3,000+ globally
- **Costs**: $200-$600/month per person
- **Included**: Office space, meeting rooms
- **Best For**: Professional services, global presence

### Industry-Specific Coworking

**RocketSpace** (San Francisco): Tech startups
**Station F** (Paris): 3,000+ startups
**The Cannon** (Houston): Energy and deep tech
**1871** (Chicago): Tech and digital

## Makerspaces

**Equipment Access**:
- 3D printers (FDM, resin)
- Laser cutters
- CNC machines
- Electronics benches
- Woodworking tools

**Costs**: $100-$200/month membership

**Active Makerspaces**:
- Noisebridge (SF): Electronics, 3D printing
- NYC Resistor (Brooklyn): Electronics
- Artisan's Asylum (Boston): Large-scale fab
- ADX (Portland): Woodworking, metal

**Best For**:
- Hardware prototyping
- Product design iteration
- Learning fabrication

## Virtual Offices

**Purpose**:
- Business address
- Mail forwarding
- Phone answering
- Conference room access

**Providers**:
- Regus Virtual: $100-$300/month
- Opus Virtual: $99-$200/month
- Davinci Virtual: $99-$149/month

**Best For**: Remote teams, compliance needs
